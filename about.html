About

My Story

I grew up with my passion for art after picking up a camera as a kid; what started as a hobby quickly turned into a passion and defined my career path.

Despite being partially blind, photography feels natural and gives me immense pleasure; it led me to an Undergraduate degree in Digital Art and a Master's in Creative Art Therapy. My disability has influenced not only my art but also my professional career. In college, I collaborated very closely with support services; I later became a student advisor in college creating accommodations plans for students with disabilities.

Combining my love of digital art and my desire to help others, I discovered that UI Design was the perfect fit for me and my skills.
