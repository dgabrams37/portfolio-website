Mallow Moon

Mallow Moon is a boutique candy store, the brand combines the lighthearted nature of candy with a clean and whimsical aesthetic, offering an experience that feels both playful and polished.

My Process

Problem Statement:

Users struggle to quickly assess whether a store has the items they need due to the lack of advanced filtering tools. The shopping process is further complicated by the inability to add multiple items to a cart and limited payment options make it less convenient.

Solution:

Mallow Moon is designed for people who prefer shopping online rather than visiting physical stores, particularly benefiting those who have difficulty finding items in traditional stores. It caters to tech-savvy users, offering a simple, clean design with easy navigation and advanced filter options. Users can easily use Mallow Moon on the go to purchase items, and have them delivered to any address.

User Testing

User Testing Results:

Users prefer an option to exit the search filters and find the "Dietary" filter vague and confusing. Users noted the need to input the billing address separately from the shipping address. Unlike the search results, the "Best Sellers" section lacked add-to-cart buttons or prices. Users suggested a confirmation popup after adding an item to the cart with an option to continue shopping. The shopping cart's item ratings were unnecessary, and users wanted additional payment options. Webpages lacked headers like "Cart" or "Home," and customers asked for more thorough product details including ingredients, allergies, and nutritional information.

Key Insights from Testing:

The suggestions significantly impacted the website design and usability by highlighting key areas for improvement.; this included missing elements like continuing shopping, a billing address, and payment methods. User flow was improved by allowing users to exit out of search results and a confirmation popup after adding items to the cart for smoother navigation. Additionally, removing unnecessary information and condensing the search results page allowed for a cleaner design. Finally, providing more detailed product information, including ingredients and nutritional details, would support informed decision-making, improving overall user experience.
